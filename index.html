About Me

Hello! I am a PhD student at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, working with Charles W. Rezk.

I am interested in algebraic topology and its applications to related fields. Some specific topics I’ve been thinking about include:

  • Power operations on multiplicative cohomology theories and their interactions, and
  • periodic families in the classical and the motivic Adams spectral sequences of the (motivic) sphere.
